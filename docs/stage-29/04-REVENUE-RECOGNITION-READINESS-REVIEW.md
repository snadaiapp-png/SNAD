# Stage 29 — Revenue Recognition Readiness Review

## Purpose

This document defines the readiness review for revenue recognition before and during controlled paid launch.

It does not claim final accounting, audit, legal, tax, or IFRS/GAAP compliance. Specialist review is required before any formal certification or external claim.

## Review Scope

```text
Contract / commercial acceptance
Subscription start date
Performance obligation assumptions
Billing cadence
Invoice timing
Payment timing
Refund and credit note policy
Deferred revenue considerations
Tax treatment assumptions
Partner revenue share if applicable
```

## Readiness Checklist

| Area | Required Review |
|---|---|
| Commercial contract | Commercial/legal review |
| Subscription period | Finance review |
| Invoice timing | Finance/tax review |
| Revenue timing | Accounting review |
| Refund/credit policy | Finance/legal review |
| Partner revenue share | Finance/legal review |
| Tax handling | Specialist review |
