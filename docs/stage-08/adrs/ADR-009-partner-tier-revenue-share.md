# ADR-009 — Partner Ecosystem Tier and Revenue Share Model

**ADR ID:** `SANAD-ADR-009`
**Date:** 2026-07-06
**Status:** APPROVED

## Context

Partner ecosystem requires tiered model with revenue share.

## Decision

Four tiers: Registered, Silver, Gold, Platinum. Tier requirements based on certified reps, customers, ARR. Commission per tier (10–20% new, 5–10% renewal). Deal registration protects opportunities for 90 days.

## Consequences

* Partners incentivized to grow.
* Conflict resolution via first-registered rule.
* Quarterly tier review.

## Alternatives

* Flat commission: rejected (no growth incentive).
* Per-deal negotiation: rejected (operational overhead).
