# SANAD Stage 08 — Publisher Governance

**Document ID:** `SANAD-ST08-MKT-PUB-001`
**Track:** 8.3
**Date:** 2026-07-06

---

## 1. Publisher Types

* Internal (SANAD team).
* Certified Partner.
* Independent Developer.
* Enterprise Publisher (private listings).

---

## 2. Onboarding Requirements

* Legal entity verification.
* Beneficial owner disclosure.
* Signed Publisher Agreement.
* Tax information.
* Bank account for settlement.
* Technical contact.
* Security contact.

---

## 3. Publisher Tier Model

| Tier      | Requirements                          | Benefits                            |
|-----------|---------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| Bronze    | Basic onboarding                      | Standard listing                    |
| Silver    | 5+ certified products, 90% SLA        | Featured placement                  |
| Gold      | 20+ products, 99% SLA, security audit | Co-marketing, priority review       |
| Platinum  | Strategic partnership                 | Custom integrations, dedicated TAM  |

---

## 4. Suspension and Termination

* Abuse: immediate suspension.
* Repeated policy violations: tier downgrade.
* Material breach: termination + refund handling.
