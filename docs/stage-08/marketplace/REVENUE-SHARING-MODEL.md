# SANAD Stage 08 — Revenue Sharing Model

**Document ID:** `SANAD-ST08-MKT-REV-001`
**Track:** 8.3
**Date:** 2026-07-06

---

## 1. Default Revenue Share

| Listing Type | SANAD Share | Publisher Share |
|--------------|-------------|-----------------|
| App          | 15%         | 85%             |
| Industry Pack| 20%         | 80%             |
| AI Agent     | 20%         | 80%             |
| Connector    | 25%         | 75%             |
| Template     | 30%         | 70%             |

---

## 2. Custom Agreements

Enterprise publishers may negotiate custom rates via Platinum tier.

---

## 3. Settlement Cycle

* Monthly settlement.
* Net 30 payment.
* Minimum payout: $100.
* Currency: USD or SAR (publisher choice).
* Tax documentation required before payout.

---

## 4. Refunds and Chargebacks

* Refund window: 14 days.
* Refunds deducted from publisher settlement.
* Chargebacks: publisher liable if product misrepresented.
