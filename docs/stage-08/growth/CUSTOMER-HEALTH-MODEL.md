## 1. Health Score Components

| Component            | Weight |
|----------------------|--------|
| Product usage        | 30%    |
| Support ticket volume| 15%    |
| NPS / CSAT           | 15%    |
| Payment status       | 15%    |
| Onboarding completion| 10%    |
| Feature adoption     | 10%    |
| Engagement (logins)  | 5%     |

---

## 2. Score Bands

| Score   | Label      | Action                                |
|---------|------------|---------------------------------------|
| 90–100  | Thriving   | Upsell motion                         |
| 70–89   | Healthy    | Nurture                               |
| 50–69   | At Risk    | CSM outreach within 5 days            |
| 30–49   | Critical   | Executive escalation                  |
| 0–29    | Churning   | Save plan + executive review          |

---

## 3. Playbooks

* At Risk: 30-minute discovery call.
* Critical: account review with PM.
* Churning: save plan with discount + executive sponsor.
