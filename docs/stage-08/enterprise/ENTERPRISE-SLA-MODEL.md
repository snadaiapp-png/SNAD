## 1. Support Tiers

| Tier      | Response Time | Channels                  |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------------|
| Standard  | 24 hours      | Email                     |
| Premium   | 4 hours       | Email + chat              |
| Enterprise| 1 hour        | Email + chat + phone      |
| Platinum  | 15 minutes    | Dedicated TAM + phone     |

---

## 2. Uptime SLA

| Tier      | Uptime     | Downtime compensation      |
|-----------|------------|----------------------------|
| Standard  | 99.5%      | 5% credit                  |
| Premium   | 99.9%      | 10% credit                 |
| Enterprise| 99.95%     | 25% credit                 |
| Platinum  | 99.99%     | 50% credit + review        |

---

## 3. Incident Communication

* SEV-1: status page update within 15 minutes.
* SEV-2: within 1 hour.
* SEV-3: within 4 hours.
* Post-incident review within 5 business days.
