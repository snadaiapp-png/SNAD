# SANAD Stage 08 — Partner Tier Model

**Document ID:** `SANAD-ST08-PART-TIER-001`
**Track:** 8.7
**Date:** 2026-07-06

---

## 1. Tiers

| Tier      | Requirements                                  | Benefits                                |
|-----------|-----------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| Registered| Basic onboarding                              | Listing in partner directory            |
| Silver    | 3 certified reps, 5 customers                 | Co-marketing, lead sharing              |
| Gold      | 10 certified reps, 25 customers, $500K ARR    | Priority leads, deal registration bonus |
| Platinum  | 25 certified reps, 100 customers, $2M ARR     | Dedicated PAM, custom integrations      |

---

## 2. Tier Review

* Quarterly review.
* Tier upgrade: meets next tier requirements for 2 consecutive quarters.
* Tier downgrade: fails to meet current tier for 2 consecutive quarters.
