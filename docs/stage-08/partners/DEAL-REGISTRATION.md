# SANAD Stage 08 — Deal Registration

**Document ID:** `SANAD-ST08-PART-DEAL-001`
**Track:** 8.7
**Date:** 2026-07-06

---

## 1. Process

1. Partner identifies opportunity.
2. Partner submits deal registration (customer, value, timeline).
3. SANAD reviews for conflict (existing deal, existing customer).
4. Approved deals protected for 90 days.
5. Partner closes deal; commission computed.
6. SANAD issues invoice; partner issues credit note.

---

## 2. Conflict Resolution

* First-registered wins.
* SANAD direct sales takes precedence only with PM approval.
* Customer can request specific partner.

---
