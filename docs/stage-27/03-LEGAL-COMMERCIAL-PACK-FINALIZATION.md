# Stage 27 — Legal and Commercial Pack Finalization

## Purpose

This document tracks the legal and commercial pack needed for first customer acquisition and partner-led implementation.

It does not claim final legal approval or certification. All external contractual use requires specialist legal review before use.

## Governing Constraints

```text
Stage 26 remains closed.
Gate 8F remains closed by governance waiver.
Final Platform Release remains GO.
Production remains LIVE.
No legal/compliance final claims without specialist review.
No production billing activation without separate approval.
```

## Required Legal Documents

| Document | Purpose | Status | External Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Terms of Service | Product usage terms | Draft required | Requires legal review |
| Privacy Policy | Privacy and data handling notice | Draft required | Requires legal review |
| Data Processing Agreement | Customer data processing terms | Draft required | Requires legal review |
| Master Services Agreement | Enterprise service terms | Draft required | Requires legal review |
| Service Level Agreement | Support and availability terms | Draft required | Requires legal review |
| Acceptable Use Policy | Usage restrictions | Draft required | Requires legal review |
| Partner Agreement | Partner duties and controls | Draft required | Requires legal review |
| Commercial Proposal Template | Pricing and scope proposal | Ready for internal draft | Requires commercial approval |

## Commercial Pack

The commercial pack should include:

1. Pilot proposal template
2. Subscription plan summary
3. Implementation scope statement
4. Partner-led delivery statement
5. Support model summary
6. Commercial assumptions
7. Exclusions
8. Approval section

## Documents Requiring Specialist Review

```text
Terms of Service
Privacy Policy
Data Processing Agreement
Master Services Agreement
Service Level Agreement
Acceptable Use Policy
Partner Agreement
```

## Internal Use Only Until Reviewed

The following must remain internal until approved through specialist review:

- Contract language
- Data protection commitments
- Compliance representations
- Service availability commitments
- Liability limitations
- Partner authority clauses
- Payment and tax clauses

## Prohibited Claims

Do not state any of the following without documented specialist review:

```text
Legally approved
Fully compliant
Certified
Final legal approval
```

## Commercial Approval Path

```text
1. Draft commercial proposal
2. Confirm pilot scope
3. Confirm pricing assumptions
4. Confirm partner involvement
5. Review legal/commercial exceptions
6. Owner approval
7. Customer presentation
```

## Risk Register

| Risk | Severity | Treatment |
|---|---|---|
| Contract used before review | High | Mark all drafts as review-required |
| Compliance overclaim | High | Prohibit final claims without evidence |
| Pricing inconsistency | Medium | Use approved proposal template |
| Partner authority confusion | Medium | Partner agreement review |
| Billing activation premature | High | Separate billing approval required |
