# Stage 26 — Operating Company Launch Plan

## Purpose

Stage 26 launches the operating company model for SNAD after completion of the post-production maturity stages 20–25. The objective is to move SNAD from a production-ready and regionally scalable platform into a structured operating company capable of market execution, customer delivery, partner management, support, revenue operations, and governance.

## Operating Company Objectives

1. Establish the commercial operating structure.
2. Define ownership and decision rights.
3. Prepare market execution governance.
4. Align customer, partner, sales, support, finance, and legal operations.
5. Convert SNAD from product readiness to company readiness.

## Company Functions

```text
- Executive Management

- Engineering and Platform Operations
- Sales and Revenue Operations
- Customer Success

- Legal and Compliance
- Security and Risk
- Support Operations
```

## Launch Phases

### Phase 1 — Operating Setup

Define roles, responsibilities, authority, policies, operating cadence, and executive decision paths.

### Phase 2 — Commercial Activation

Prepare pipeline ownership, sales motion, pilot conversion, pricing execution, billing readiness, and partner participation.

### Phase 3 — Delivery Readiness

Create onboarding procedures, support handoff, partner implementation playbook, and customer success operating model.

### Phase 4 — Market Execution

Run first market campaigns, partner outreach, enterprise demos, and first customer onboarding.
