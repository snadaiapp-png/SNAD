# Stage 26 — KPI and Executive Reporting

## Purpose

This document defines the KPI and executive reporting model for SNAD operating company launch and market execution.

The objective is to provide disciplined visibility into commercial performance, customer success, product quality, support operations, revenue readiness, partner performance, and governance risk.

## Reporting Cadence

| Report | Cadence | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Executive Operating Report | Weekly | Executive Owner |
| Sales Pipeline Report | Weekly | Sales Operations |
| Customer Success Report | Weekly | Customer Success |
| Support Operations Report | Weekly | Support Lead |
| Product & Engineering Report | Bi-weekly | Product/Engineering |
| Risk & Compliance Report | Monthly | Governance Owner |
| Revenue Operations Report | Monthly | Revenue Operations |
