# Stage 19 — Partner Recruitment Program

**Date**: 2026-07-08
**Status**: READY

---

## Partner Types

```
1. Implementation Partners
   Role: Help customers implement and customize SNAD
   Target: IT consulting firms, system integrators in Saudi/GCC
   Revenue: Implementation fee (paid by customer) + 10% referral commission

2. Integration Partners
   Role: Build integrations between SNAD and other platforms
   Target: Software companies (Slack, QuickBooks, Zapier equivalents)
   Revenue: Revenue share (negotiated, often co-marketing)

3. Consulting Partners
   Role: Provide strategic consulting on business processes using SNAD
   Target: Management consulting firms
   Revenue: Consulting fee + 5% referral commission

4. Sales Partners (Resellers)
   Role: Sell SNAD subscriptions to their customers
   Target: IT resellers, VARs in Saudi/GCC
   Revenue: 20% reseller discount + 10-15% commission

5. Marketplace Partners
   Role: Build and sell extensions on SNAD Marketplace
   Target: ISVs, freelance developers
   Revenue: 70/30 or 80/20 revenue share
```

## Acceptance Criteria

```
General criteria (all partners):
  1. Legal entity (company registration in Saudi/GCC)
  2. Good reputation (references checked)
  3. Financial stability
  4. No conflicts of interest
  5. Signed partnership agreement

Type-specific criteria:
  Implementation: 2+ certified SNAD consultants, implementation track record
  Integration: Stable product, API capability, security review
  Consulting: Business consulting expertise, industry experience
  Sales: B2B sales team, customer base in target segments, 5+ customers/year commitment
  Marketplace: Technical capability, quality review passed
```

## Partner Levels

```
Tier 1: Registered Partner
  Requirements: Signed agreement, basic training
  Benefits:
    - Access to partner portal
    - Basic training materials
    - Referral commission (5%)
    - Email support

Tier 2: Certified Partner
  Requirements: 2+ certified consultants, 2+ implementations
  Benefits:
    - All Tier 1 benefits
    - Higher commission (10%)
    - Priority support
    - Co-marketing opportunities
    - Lead sharing

Tier 3: Premier Partner
  Requirements: Strategic relationship, $100k+ annual commitment
  Benefits:
    - All Tier 2 benefits
    - Highest commission (15%)
    - Dedicated partner manager
    - Joint roadmap planning
    - Exclusive territories (optional)
```

## Qualification Process

```
1. Partner applies (partner portal or direct outreach)
2. SNAD reviews application (legal, financial, technical)
3. Initial call (30 min, mutual fit assessment)
4. Technical evaluation (for implementation/integration partners)
5. Partnership agreement signed
6. Onboarding (training, portal access, collateral)
7. First engagement (shadow or co-sell)
8. Performance review (quarterly)

Timeline: 2-4 weeks from application to signed agreement
```

## Revenue Model

```
Implementation Partners:
  - Implementation fee: $5,000-$20,000 per project (paid by customer)
  - Referral commission: 10% of first-year subscription revenue
  - Example: Customer pays $490/year (annual Professional)
    Partner receives: $49 referral + implementation fee

Integration Partners:
  - Revenue share: Negotiated (typically 50/50 on integration sales)
  - Co-marketing: Joint campaigns
  - Example: Integration sold for $50/month
    SNAD receives: $25/month
    Partner receives: $25/month

Consulting Partners:
  - Consulting fee: $100-$200/hour (paid by customer)
  - Referral commission: 5% of first-year subscription
  - Example: Customer pays $490/year
    Partner receives: $24.50 referral + consulting fee

Sales Partners (Resellers):
  - Reseller discount: 20% off list price
  - Commission: 10-15% of discounted price
  - Example: Professional $49/month
    Reseller pays: $39.20/month (20% off)
    Reseller charges customer: $49/month
    Reseller profit: $9.80/month + SNAD commission

Marketplace Partners:
  - Revenue share: 70/30 (developer/SNAD) for modules
  - Revenue share: 80/20 (creator/SNAD) for templates/themes
  - Example: Extension sold for $50/month
    Developer receives: $33.50/month (after fees + 30% platform fee)
    SNAD receives: $16.50/month
```

## Partner Obligations

```
All partners:
  - Maintain certification (annual renewal)
  - Maintain customer satisfaction (NPS > 30)
  - Comply with SNAD brand guidelines
  - No disparaging SNAD publicly
  - Report customer feedback to SNAD
  - No unauthorized discounting
  - Compliance with laws (including PDPL)

Implementation partners:
  - Minimum 2 certified consultants
  - Complete implementations per SNAD methodology
  - Report implementation status monthly
  - Escalate issues to SNAD support

Sales partners:
  - Minimum 5 customers/year commitment
  - Accurate representation of SNAD capabilities
  - No false promises to customers
  - Use SNAD-provided sales collateral
```

## SNAD Obligations

```
To all partners:
  - Provide partner portal access
  - Provide training materials and certification program
  - Provide sales collateral (bilingual)
  - Provide technical documentation
  - Respond to partner support requests within 48 hours
  - Quarterly partner business review

To Certified/Premier partners:
  - Lead sharing (qualified leads in partner's territory)
  - Co-marketing budget (co-approved campaigns)
  - Early access to product roadmap
  - Dedicated partner manager (Premier)
  - Joint customer engagement support
```
