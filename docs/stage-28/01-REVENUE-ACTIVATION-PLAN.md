# Stage 28 — Revenue Activation Plan

## Purpose

This document defines the controlled revenue activation plan for SNAD after Stage 27 closure.

Stage 28 prepares the organization, product, billing governance, commercial motion, and operational controls required to convert the first paid customer without reopening prior production gates or activating live payments without separate explicit approval.


## Revenue Activation Objective

Prepare SNAD for first controlled paid revenue by aligning:

- Commercial offer
- Subscription packaging
- Billing approval gate
- Invoice/tax readiness
- Customer conversion path
- Revenue operations reporting
- Renewal and expansion motion

## Activation Principles

```text
No live billing activation without separate approval.
No cardholder data storage in SNAD systems.
No tax/legal certification claim without specialist review.
No production credential exposure.
No high-impact AI decision without human confirmation.
```

| Workstream | Output |
|---|---|
