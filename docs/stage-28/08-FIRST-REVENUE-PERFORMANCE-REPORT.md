# Stage 28 — First Revenue Performance Report

## Purpose

This document defines the reporting template for Stage 28 revenue activation and first paid customer conversion readiness.

The report is used to summarize execution status, commercial readiness, billing governance, subscription readiness, invoice/tax readiness, renewal/expansion signals, risks, and Stage 29 recommendation.

## Reporting Cadence

```text
Weekly during Stage 28 execution
Final report at Stage 28 closure
```

## Executive Summary

Required fields:

```text
Stage status
Production status
First paid customer readiness
Billing activation status
Revenue readiness score
Top risks
Decisions required
Next stage recommendation
```

## Revenue Activation Summary

Track:

- Revenue activation plan status
- Offer approval status
- First paid customer candidate status
- Commercial approval path
- Billing approval gate
- Production impact

## First Paid Conversion Summary

Track:

```text
Customer status
Conversion stage
Sponsor status
Scope status
Commercial proposal status
Legal/tax review status
Billing gate status
Customer success owner
```

## Billing and Subscription Summary

Track:

- Billing activation approval status
- Stripe/billing configuration readiness
- Subscription lifecycle state
- Invoice/tax readiness
- Secret handling status
- Webhook readiness status

## Renewal and Expansion Summary

Track:

```text
Customer health
Activation score
Adoption score
Expansion signals
Renewal risk
Executive engagement
Customer success actions
```

## Risk Register Summary

Minimum risk areas:

- Premature live billing
- Secret exposure
- Tax readiness gap
- Legal review blocker
- Customer payment friction
- Support capacity gap
- Partner scope confusion

## Decision Log

```text
Decision
Owner
Date
Evidence
Status
Follow-up action
```

## Stage 29 Recommendation

Recommended next stage after Stage 28:

```text
Stage 29 — Controlled Paid Launch & Revenue Operations Execution
```

Recommended Stage 29 scope:

1. Controlled live billing activation approval
2. First paid customer subscription activation
3. Payment and invoice event verification
4. Revenue recognition readiness review
5. Customer payment support operations
6. Renewal and expansion execution
7. Revenue operations monitoring
8. First paid launch closure record
