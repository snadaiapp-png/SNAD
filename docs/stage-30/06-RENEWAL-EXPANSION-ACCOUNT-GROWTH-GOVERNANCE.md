# 06 — Renewal, Expansion, and Account Growth Governance

## Purpose

This document defines how SNAD governs renewal, expansion, upsell, cross-sell, and account growth for first paid customers.

Expansion must be tied to verified value and must not create unsupported commitments.

## Growth Principles

```text
Renewal is earned through value realization.
Expansion must be governed and evidence-based.
Discounts and exceptions require approval.
Product commitments must not bypass roadmap governance.
AI recommendations may inform but not autonomously decide commercial action.
```

## Renewal Readiness Model

| Review Point | Objective |
| --- | --- |
| Activation + 30 days | Confirm initial value and adoption health. |
| Mid-cycle review | Identify risks, support blockers, and success gaps. |
| Pre-renewal review | Confirm value evidence, billing status, and stakeholder alignment. |
| Renewal decision | Confirm commercial and operational readiness. |

## Expansion Eligibility

An account may be considered for expansion only when:

```text
Core adoption is healthy.
Support incidents are stable or resolved.
Billing and collection status is current.
Customer value evidence exists.
Stakeholder engagement is active.
Requested expansion aligns with supported SNAD modules.
Security/privacy implications are reviewed when needed.
```

## Expansion Types

| Type | Example | Required Governance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Seat expansion | More users | Subscription scope approval |
| Module expansion | CRM to ERP or HRM | Customer success + revenue approval |
| Workflow expansion | Additional automated process | Workflow fit review |
| AI capability expansion | AI assistance or analytics | AI governance review when material |
| Industry configuration | Sector-specific setup | Product/operations review |

## Renewal Risk Signals

```text
Low usage.
Repeated unresolved support issues.
Billing disputes or overdue payments.
Sponsor disengagement.
Unmet onboarding objectives.
High customization pressure.
Security/privacy concerns.
```

## Expansion Prohibited Without Approval

```text
Unapproved discount or commercial exception.
Unsupported custom feature commitment.
Unreviewed sensitive data integration.
High-impact AI automation without human confirmation.
Expansion that creates operational support load without owner assignment.
```
