# 02 — Customer Success Operating Model

## Purpose

This document defines the customer success operating model for first paid SNAD customers.

The goal is to ensure that customer activation converts into measurable adoption, business value, renewal readiness, and controlled expansion opportunities.

## Operating Principles

```text
Customer success is proactive, not reactive.
Customer value must be traceable.
Usage signals must be reviewed with human judgment.
Commercial expansion must be governed.
Support incidents must feed success reviews.
No AI recommendation may trigger high-impact customer decisions without human confirmation.
```

## Customer Success Ownership

| Role | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| Customer Success Owner | Owns customer adoption, value realization, and account health. |
| Support Owner | Owns tickets, SLA response, and escalation management. |
| Revenue Owner | Owns billing status, renewal readiness, and commercial controls. |
| Product Owner | Reviews product feedback and roadmap signals. |
| Security / Compliance Reviewer | Reviews sensitive data, integration, or compliance-impacting requests when required. |

## Customer Success Cadence


```text

Renewal readiness: INITIALIZED
```

## Account Health Model

| Dimension | Green | Amber | Red |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adoption | Active usage aligns with plan | Incomplete or inconsistent usage | No meaningful usage |
| Support | Tickets low and resolved on time | Repeated tickets or unresolved blockers | Critical unresolved issues |
| Billing | Current and reconciled | Pending clarification | Overdue or disputed |
| Stakeholder engagement | Decision-maker engaged | Limited engagement | No sponsor engagement |
| Value realization | Outcomes visible | Outcomes unclear | No value evidence |

## Escalation Triggers

```text
Customer cannot complete core workflow.
Billing dispute or collection failure occurs.
Security/privacy concern is raised.
Critical support issue remains unresolved beyond SLA.
Customer sponsor disengages.
Usage drops below onboarding plan.
AI-generated recommendation is considered for a high-impact decision.
```
