# Stage 18 — Growth Channels Strategy

**Date**: 2026-07-08

---

## Growth Channels

### 1. Direct Sales

```
Target: Enterprise customers ($199+/month)
Approach: Outbound sales (account-based marketing)

Process:
  - Identify target accounts (ICP: 100-1,000 employees, GCC)
  - Research decision makers (CTO, CFO, COO)
  - Cold outreach (email, LinkedIn)
  - Discovery call
  - Product demo
  - Proposal
  - Contract negotiation
  - Onboarding

Team: 1-2 sales reps (future hire)
Cost: $5,000-$10,000 per acquisition
Success metric: 2-3 enterprise customers per quarter
Timeline: 3-6 months per deal
```

### 2. Content Marketing

```
Target: Professional tier customers ($49/month)
Approach: Inbound marketing (attract via content)

Content types:
  - Blog posts (business operations, productivity, SNAD features)
  - Case studies (pilot customer success stories)
  - Whitepapers (bilingual business in Saudi Arabia)
  - Video tutorials (how-to guides)
  - Webinars (monthly, Arabic + English)
  - Social media (LinkedIn, Twitter/X)

Languages: Arabic (primary), English (secondary)
Frequency: 2-3 blog posts per week, 1 webinar per month
Cost: $500-$2,000/month (content creation)
Success metric: 50+ leads per month, 5% conversion to trial
Timeline: 3-6 months to see traction
```

### 3. Partnerships

```
Target: All tiers
Approach: Leverage partner networks

Partner channels:
  - Implementation partners (consulting firms)
  - Integration partners (Slack, QuickBooks, etc.)
  - Reseller partners (IT VARs)
  - Referral partners (existing customers)

Process:
  - Recruit partners (Stage 18 partner model)
  - Enable partners (training, collateral)
  - Co-market (joint webinars, content)
  - Track referrals

Cost: Revenue share (5-15%)
Success metric: 20% of new customers from partners
Timeline: 6-12 months to build partner channel
```

### 4. Referrals

```
Target: All tiers
Approach: Customer referral program

Program:
  - Existing customer refers new customer
  - New customer signs up for paid plan
  - Referring customer gets 1 month free (or $50 credit)
  - Referred customer gets 10% off first 3 months

Tracking: Referral codes in CRM
Promotion: In-app banner, email campaign
Cost: 1 month revenue per referral
Success metric: 15% of new customers from referrals
Timeline: Immediate (once program launched)
```

### 5. Marketplace

```
Target: All tiers
Approach: Attract via marketplace extensions

Strategy:
  - Build key integrations (Slack, Zapier, QuickBooks)
  - Attract third-party developers
  - Extensions drive platform adoption
  - Customers come for extension, stay for platform

Cost: Development cost for SNAD-built extensions
Success metric: 30% of new customers cite marketplace as factor
Timeline: 12+ months (after marketplace launch)
```

### 6. Industry Verticals

```
Target: Specific industries
Approach: Industry-specific marketing

Verticals:
  - Professional services (consulting, legal, accounting)
  - Retail (chains, e-commerce)
  - Manufacturing (small to mid-sized)
  - Healthcare (clinics, small hospitals)
  - Construction (contractors)

Tactics:
  - Industry-specific landing pages
  - Industry case studies
  - Industry conferences and events
  - Industry publications

Cost: $1,000-$3,000 per vertical per month
Success metric: 10+ customers per vertical per quarter
Timeline: 6-12 months per vertical
```

### 7. Digital Campaigns

```
Target: Professional tier
Approach: Paid digital marketing

Channels:
  - Google Ads (search: "business operating system", "ERP Saudi Arabia")
  - LinkedIn Ads (target: decision makers in GCC)
  - Facebook/Instagram (brand awareness)
  - Twitter/X (thought leadership)

Budget: $2,000-$5,000/month
Cost per lead: $20-$50
Success metric: 100+ leads per month, 10% conversion to trial
Timeline: Immediate (campaigns can start now)
```

### 8. Enterprise Relations

```
Target: Large enterprise customers
Approach: Strategic relationships

Tactics:
  - Government relations (Saudi Vision 2030 alignment)
  - Chamber of commerce memberships
  - Industry association memberships
  - Executive events and conferences
  - Thought leadership (speaking, publishing)

Cost: $5,000-$10,000/year (memberships, events)
Success metric: 2-3 enterprise customers per year
Timeline: 12+ months (relationship building)
```
